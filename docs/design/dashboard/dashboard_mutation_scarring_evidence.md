# Dashboard Mutation Scarring Evidence

This document explains how localized environmental degradation (Mutation Scarring) is integrated into the meta-strategic Domain Dashboard snapshots.

## Overview

The `domain_dashboard_snapshot_builder` has been updated to aggregate evidence of the Tower's physical reaction to established order. This ensures that the environmental "stain" caused by territorial footholds—including decaying and high-visibility claims—is auditably tracked in the meta-strategic oversight layer.

## Integrated Evidence

The dashboard snapshot now includes a `mutation_scarring_summary` containing:

1.  **Scarred Nodes Count**: A count of all unique room nodes that have established strategic Order.
2.  **Highest Scar Intensity**: Surfaces the peak environmental degradation currently active among established footholds.
3.  **Aggregate Hazard Bias**: A material sum of the tactical difficulty and mutation probability escalation generated by all active scars.

## Strategic Cognition

Surfacing scarring intensity in the dashboard forces the player to acknowledge the physical price of their territorial ambition. A player who builds many high-visibility outposts will see their "highest scar intensity" increase, signaling a progressive increase in tactical friction and environmental risk in those areas.

## Identity Preservation

*   **Order Leaves a Mark**: The dashboard surfaces the "net stain" of the player's journey, reinforcing the principle that rewards carry lasting environmental consequences.
*   **Hostility Preservation**: The dashboard tracks whether established order is correctly attracting more visibility and higher mutation risk through localized scarring.
*   **Operational Uncertainty**: The dashboard derive information only from *known* footholds and *current* state. It does not reveal hidden "future scars" or unexplored topographical risks.
